About

SerraSense is a free companion app that lets you monitor temperature and humidity inside your greenhouse in real time. It connects over your local network to your own ESP32-based sensor device and shows live readings, analytics, VPD (vapor pressure deficit) and dew-point risk metrics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SerraSense free?
Yes. The app is completely free. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no paid content.
How do I connect my sensor?
In Settings, enter the local IP address of your ESP32 sensor device. Make sure your iPhone or iPad is on the same Wi-Fi network as the sensor.
The app shows no data — what should I do?
Check that the sensor device is powered on and reachable on your local network, and that the IP address in Settings is correct. Grant the app permission to access the local network when prompted.
What do VPD and dew-point risk mean?
VPD (vapor pressure deficit) reflects how favorable the air is for plant transpiration. Dew-point risk indicates when condensation may form, which can encourage mold.
Do you collect my data?
No. Sensor readings stay on your device and are read directly from your own hardware over the local network.
